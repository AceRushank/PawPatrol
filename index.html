Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safer environment by providing a platform for reporting stray dog attacks. Through this initiative, we aim to protect both people and animals from harm. By collecting and sharing information about stray dog incidents, we can help communities take proactive measures to prevent future attacks.

Raising awareness about the issue of stray dogs is also a crucial part of our mission. We believe that by educating the public, we can foster a greater understanding and compassion towards stray dogs. This can lead to more effective solutions that ensure the safety and well-being of everyone in our communities.
